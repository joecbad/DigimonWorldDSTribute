Digimon World DS

Digimon World DS is a video game within the Digimon franchise for the Nintendo DS!


Digimon Word DS is one of my favorite video games. My earliest encounter with Digimon was when I was at a used bookstore with my Dad when I was very young, and I saw a book with Digimon on it. I just thought the pictures and the descriptions looked really cool, especially a walrus-like Digimon Ikkakumon and his digivolution Zudomon. Soon after that my Dad also got me a copy of Digimon World DS, which I played but I didn't get very far. I don't exactly remember but I think I went down a wrong way in the level, and when I hit a dead end I kept trying to find a different way to go but I couldn't find any other way. I kept trying until I eventually gave up. That was until many years ago when I decided to try at the game again, and I was so engrossed by it and it officially became one of my favorite games of all time.

Digimon World DS Facts

      Although the game is titled Digimon World, the game is actually the first installment of the Digimon Story series, which focuses less on virtual pet gameplay and more on JRPG gameplay.
      It was released nationally in late 2006.
      The main antagonist of the game, the mysterious "Unknown-D," seeks to eliminate all humans, as they are seen as dangerous to Digimon.
      The game has a hub-world style gameplay, with the player going to different levels and then back to the main area.
      There are 300 different Digimon in Digimon World DS!
